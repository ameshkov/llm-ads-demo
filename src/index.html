Timeline

Large Language Models have not been actively used by adtech companies besides some initial experiments. However, everyone sees the potential and we believe they are soon arise.

  1. 2021

    SmarterChild: pioneering AI chatbot

    Early chatbot SmarterChild gains popularity among preteens for its witty responses on AIM and MSN. It is a precursor to modern AI bots like Siri and Alexa, even inspiring investors to fund Siri.

  2. December 2015

    Creation of OpenAI

    OpenAI is introduced as a non-profit organization focused on advancing digital intelligence in a way that benefits humanity as a whole.

  3. June 2018

    GPT-1 launch

    OpenAI unveils GPT-1, its initial version of GPT.

  4. February 2019

    Launch of GPT-2

    The second version of GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer), known as GPT-2, makes its debut.

  5. July 2019

    Partnership between Microsoft and OpenAI

    The partnership between Microsoft and OpenAI is disclosed, marking the commencement of their collaborative efforts in advancing AI technology.

  6. June 2020

    GPT-3 launch

    OpenAI introduces GPT-3, which has over a thousand times the number of parameters compared to its initial predecessor.

  7. November 2022

    OpenAI launches GPT-3.5 featuring ChatGPT

    OpenAI introduces GPT-3, which has over a thousand times the number of parameters compared to its initial predecessor.
